Auction Connect: Online Auction Learning Launch Plan

Experiment 1

Concept: Online Auction for Unique Antique Items

Critical Assumptions

  • People trust the platform to sell and buy unique antique items.
  • It provides an easy and trustworthy buying experience.

Timeframe

2 Months

Sample Size

Less than 1000 people in Tier 1 cities

Experiment Design

  1. Create a prototype with listing, bidding, and transaction features.
  2. Host a limited auction with curated antique items.
  3. Monitor user actions, gather feedback, and observe transaction rates.

Who?

Sellers

Recruited from local antique shops and collectors' communities

Buyers

Targeted through social media and antique enthusiast forums

Tracked Metrics

  • Number of registered users
  • Percentage of auctions receiving bids
  • Average number of bids per item

Data to be Recorded

Comprehensive data throughout the test phase

What to Watch For

Confirming Data

Sellers report ease of listing and satisfaction.

Disconfirming Data

Buyers abandon auctions due to complexity or lack of interest.
